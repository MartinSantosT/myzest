## Why These Creators Are Ideal for Zest

Zest is a self-hosted recipe manager application that aligns perfectly with the content these creators produce:

- **Docker-Native**: Zest can be deployed as a Docker container, matching the containerization focus of these channels
- **Open Source & Self-Hosting**: Core values of the entire creator community
- **Privacy-Focused**: Users maintain control of their recipe data on their own infrastructure
- **Practical Application**: Unlike infrastructure-only tools, Zest provides immediate utility to homelab enthusiasts
- **Family-Friendly**: Recipe management has universal appeal for homelab users managing family infrastructure

## Outreach Strategy Recommendations

### Tier 1 Creators (60K-500K subscribers)
- **Best Approach**: Direct partnerships or feature in dedicated videos
- **Potential**: Could result in 5-15K viewers per feature
- **Contact**: Use YouTube community posts, email (often found in channel descriptions), or official websites

### Tier 2 Creators (50K-400K subscribers)
- **Best Approach**: App review videos, sponsorship mentions, or guest appearances on podcasts
- **Potential**: Highly engaged audiences with strong interest in self-hosting
- **Contact**: Patreon support, email, social media mentions, or forum engagement

### Tier 3 Creators (2M+ subscribers)
- **Best Approach**: Press release, sponsorship packages, or high-level partnership discussions
- **Potential**: Massive reach but lower conversion rate due to broader audience
- **Contact**: Official business contact forms, manager contacts, or sponsorship inquiries

## Notes for Outreach

1. **Timing**: February-March is good for spring cleaning/family project content
2. **Authenticity**: Many creators pride themselves on honest reviews - ensure Zest is genuinely ready for featured
3. **Documentation**: Provide clear, concise Docker deployment instructions
4. **Support**: Be responsive to creator questions about Zest features and configuration
5. **Smaller Channels**: Don't overlook Tier 2 & 3 creators - they often have more engaged audiences and are more likely to respond
6. **Community First**: These creators value community feedback - engage authentically on platforms before reaching out
